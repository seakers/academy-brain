Main requirements of the avionics subsystem

• Throughput (instructions per second)
• Data storage
  • Firmware (ROM) – kb
  • O/S data (RAM) – Mb
  • Data storage (Disk) – Gb
• Radiation hardness (10krad LEO, 100krad GEO, ~Mrad interplanetary)
• Reliability/fault tolerance
• Flexibility: change after launch
