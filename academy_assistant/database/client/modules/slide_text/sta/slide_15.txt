Photovoltaic power generation

• Arrays of solar cells
    • p-n junctions, like photodetectors
    • Transform photons into electrons using photovoltaic effect
• Only viable if close enough to the Sun
    • up to Mars or even Jupiter
    • cf. the Juno mission got 200W power with a 60m^2 solar array
• High specific power 25-300 W/kg
• Configurations
    • Body-mounted (cheapest) vs deployable
    • Fixed (cheapest) vs 1-axis vs 2-axis gimbal
        • Use sun sensors for pointing
