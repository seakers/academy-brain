Solar cells

• Main requirement and cost driver is the solar cell efficiency
  • Ratio of output power density \( S_{out} \left[ \frac{W}{m^2} \right] \) to input power density \( S_{in} \left[ \frac{W}{m^2} \right] \)
    \[
    \eta = \frac{S_{out}}{S_{in}}
    \]
    • \( S_{in} \approx 1368 \, W/m^2 \) in earth's vicinity

• Types:
  • Silicon
    • Old, cheap, lots of heritage
    • Low efficiency \( \eta \approx 15\% \)
  • Gallium Arsenide
    • Slightly better than Si
    • Efficiency \( \eta \approx 20\% \)
  • Multi-junction
    • State of the art
    • Several semiconductor layers
    • \( \eta \approx 30 - 35\% \)
