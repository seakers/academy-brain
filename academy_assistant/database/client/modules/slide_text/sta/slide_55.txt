Slide text: 
- In thermal equilibrium, the rate of heat flow going in equals that going out
- Power in: $\dot{Q}_{in} = \dot{Q}_{sun} + \dot{Q}_{alb} + \dot{Q}_{Earth} + \dot{Q}_{int}$
  - Direct solar irradiance
  - Reflected sunlight (Albedo)
  - Thermal emission from Earth
  - Internal heat sources
- Power out: $\dot{Q}_{out} = \dot{Q}_{out}(T)$
  - Radiation to space, depends on surface temperature
- Thermal balance equation leads to equilibrium temperature
  $\dot{Q}_{in} = \dot{Q}_{out}(T)$
