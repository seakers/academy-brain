- Expected static load envelopes are provided in launch vehicle user guides for axial and lateral.
- Usually in \( g \).
- Example from Falcon 9 Launch Vehicle User Guide. The spacecraft must be designed to be able to withstand any combination of loads inside this polygon.
- Other considerations
  - Center of gravity < 180in
  - Lateral CG offset < 5in
  - Mass between 1810 and 9070kg
