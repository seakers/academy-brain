Main requirements of the avionics subsystem
- Throughput (instructions per second)
- Data storage
  - Firmware (ROM) – kb
  - O/S data (RAM) – Mb
  - Data storage (Disk) – Gb
- Radiation hardness (10krad min for LEO, 100krad for GEO, ~Mrad for interplanetary missions)
- Reliability/fault tolerance
- Flexibility: update flight software after launch
