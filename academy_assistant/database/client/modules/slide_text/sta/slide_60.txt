Reflected sunlight

\[
\dot{Q}_{alb} = \Phi_s \cdot R \cdot \alpha_{VNIR} \cdot A_{Earth}
\]

- Where \( R \) is the albedo (fraction of solar irradiance reflected off Earth surface)
  - Function of orbit and spacecraft attitude
  - Values of Earth albedo oscillate significantly between 0.2-0.6
- \( A_{Earth} \) is the area perpendicular to spacecraft-Earth vector
- Example: \( \alpha_{VNIR} = 0.4 \), \( R = 0.4 \), \( A_{Earth} = 1m^2 \), we get \( \dot{Q}_{alb} = 219W \)
