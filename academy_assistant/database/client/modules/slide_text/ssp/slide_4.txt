NASA Orbiting Carbon Observatory mission

• OCO lost at launch in 2/2009 (Taurus).
• OCO-2 successfully launched 7/2014 (Delta-II)
• Spacecraft: 454kg, 521W
• Provides measurements of column averaged $CO_2$ air mole fraction on regional scales
• Payload: 3 high-resolution grating spectrometers
  • $\lambda = 2.06\mu m, 1.61\mu m, 0.765\mu m$
• 710km, 16-day repeat cycle, SSO PM orbit (A-train)
