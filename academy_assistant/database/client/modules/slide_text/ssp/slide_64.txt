MW radiometers

• MW radiometers are very sensitive antennas that measure the intensity of EM radiation coming from
    • Atmosphere (gases, clouds, precipitation)
    • Land or ocean surface (soil moisture, ocean salinity, temperature)
• Main Figures of Merit:
    • Spatial resolution
    • Swath
    • Sensitivity
• Main design parameters:
    • Aperture
    • Frequency
    • Bandwidth/integration time
