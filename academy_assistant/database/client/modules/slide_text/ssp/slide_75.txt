Why space-based radar?

- Better spatial resolution than radiometers
- Applications:
  - Surface topography
  - Oceanography
  - Precipitation
  - Vegetation structure
  - Ice sheet topography
  - Wind
- Types
  - Real vs Synthetic Aperture
  - Altimeter
  - Scatterometer
  - Polarimeter
  - Doppler
